2021 Tokyo Olympics Visualisation

The visualisation has 3 parts.
Firstly a world heatmap visualising top medalist countries worldwide with red color.,
Secondly, a detailed bar chart with a filter showing number of medals for different countries.
Lastly, an analyzation between Female and Male athlete by using pie chart and normalized bar chart.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Worldwide Total Medal Heatmap

The map visualisation displays the number of medals obtained by each country. The tooltip shows the country name and total number of medals. The level of color shows the number of medals,the reder the color is the more metals a country has.

United state of America and China have got the most number of medals in 2021 Tokyo Olympics.

In Asia, Russia and China dominate on the number of medals. In North America, apparently U.S has got more medals than Canada. In South America, Brazil and Chile stand out worldwide. In Pacific, Australia is the only country and it has top 5 medal numbers hence it is so obvious. In the European, every country seems not so outstanding because they are small country in size and they do not have a lot of medals to stand out.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Worldwide Countries Total Medal Bar Chart

The bar chart has imeplemented a minimum of 10 medals filter.


Top medal country from each continent is shown with text annotation of orange except Japan (The host).Japan (The host).


By moving the slider, the bar chart changes dynamically according to the minimum number of medals filter.


When mouse is toggled on top of the bar chart, a tooltip contains country name, number of Gold medals, number of Silver Medals, number of Bronze medals and total number of medals will show.
